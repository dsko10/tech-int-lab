O mnie

Damian started playing motorsport games in the early 2000’s with titles such as Official Formula One Racing, F1 Racing Championship, and Grand Prix 4.

He then discovered the world of online racing, where he then moved to F1 Challenge 99-02 with a community mod to put 2007 cars and tracks into the game, and he did his first online race back in 2007 on August 14th.

After a short period with that title, Damian moved to rFactor 1 in the early parts of 2008.

He bought his first steering wheel and continued to race on the platform until 2014 when he decided to step down from sim racing, to pursue an esports career in non-motorsport games.

Damian made his comeback into sim racing in mid 2020, after a friend of his convinced him to do so, and after a successful campaign in the 2021 Formula SimRacing Academy series, where he claimed the driver’s title, he joined Burst Esport at the end of that year.

Damian is a mature driver, who has a great balance of being aggressive and staying patient to wait for the right moment. He’s taken a big step from the FSR Academy series, up to the World Championship division in 2022. The Pole has the talent and his biggest achievements are still yet to come.

Damian Skowron, Informatyka NST, 6 semestr
